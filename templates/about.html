In today's competitive market, predicting the price of laptops based on their specifications and trends is vital for both consumers and retailers. This project, LappyPricer, leverages data science techniques to develop a robust model capable of forecasting laptop prices using key features such as brand, processor type, RAM, storage, screen size, and other specifications.

The system utilizes a comprehensive dataset collected from various online retailers, which is then preprocessed and analyzed through exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover patterns and relationships between different features and pricing. By applying machine learning algorithms such as linear regression, decision trees, and random forest, the model aims to accurately predict laptop prices, enabling consumers to make informed purchasing decisions and retailers to optimize their pricing strategies.

In the modern digital age, laptops have become an essential tool for individuals and businesses alike. Accurate price forecasting based on laptop features can bridge the gap between consumer expectations and market trends. LappyPricer helps both consumers and sellers make better, data-driven decisions.
